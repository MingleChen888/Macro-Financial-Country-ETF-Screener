# Product Brief: Macro-Financial Country ETF Screener

## User-facing purpose

This product helps a junior finance or strategy analyst compare selected countries using both economic fundamentals and investable ETF market signals.

## Main takeaway

Japan (EWJ) has the highest combined macro-financial score at 74.3 out of 100. China (MCHI) has the lowest score at 26.4. The top result is driven by the combination of macro fundamentals and ETF risk-return characteristics, not by one single indicator.

## Diversification note

The strongest monthly ETF return correlation is DEU (EWG) and GBR (EWU) (0.88). The weakest pairwise correlation is CHN (MCHI) and IND (INDA) (0.15). Lower correlations can be useful for diversification, but this should be checked with longer samples and additional risk factors.
